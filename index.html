Connecting to your Data

Ready to connecto your data inside Designer? With an active license, a business problem, and a few relevant data sources, you can start building your workflow. Check out these resources to get started!

Results Window

Want to see your data but not done building your workflow? Alteryx Designer has a Results window located below the canvas. Select a tool to see the format of the data going in. Run the workflow to see the data output from the tool, as well as data quality indicators, messages, errors, and links to output files. For more help intepreting results, check out the Results Window Help page!

Got stuck?

Hit a snag building your workflow? Our global search checks Alteryx tools, Gallery items, Help content, and Community posts to find you the best information. Click Search in the top right corner of the Designer interface to get started!
